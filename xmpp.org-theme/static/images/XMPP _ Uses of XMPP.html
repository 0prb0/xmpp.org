Uses of XMPP

It's typical for people unfamiliar with the myriad uses of XMPP to pigeonhole it into being a protocol used for instant messaging.

While that's a huge portion of XMPP traffic on the Internet, XMPP has a variety of other uses, including:

  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • WebRTC
  • Social
  • Realtime Web
